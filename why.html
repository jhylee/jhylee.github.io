Why Coastline Market?

At Coastline, we're building an online marketplace that is direct, local and transparent. Some benefits of our model include:

1. Empower fishers to earn a living wage while supporting local economies

2. Enjoy the freshest boat-to-plate seafood

3. Reduce the environmental impacts of transporting and preserving catch amongst middlemen

4. Increased transparency in the supply chain on who, when, where your seafood came from
