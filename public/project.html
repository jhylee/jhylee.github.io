VoiceBook

Voicebook is an integrated radio + social media feed station which simply plays your latest hits when your social feeds are offline, but it transforms into an automated social media aggregator/reader when your social profiles begin to receive new Facebook posts, tweets, and Instagram uploads. In this even, Voicebook will read (out loud) your social interactions in real time as they happen. This text-to-speech feature isn't limited to text and will actually analyze and verbally interpret photos for the user. Finally, an intelligent voice recognition system enables users to act on these social updates (such as commenting or liking) using their voice, without ever taking their eyes off the road.

Query

Query is a real-time classroom collaboration tool designed for the university lecture. Using a unique joining code, professors can create a virtual classroom where students can ask questions and also up/downvote their peers questions. Once a student asks a questions, all other students on the platform are prompted by a modal, asking if the question is a good one or not. If this question meets a certain threshold of "yes" responses, it is displayed on the lecturer's projector screen, which he can choose to address then/at a later time. These questions can be answered through laptop, phone, or through a pebble application. As these questions are submitted, a list of curated questions are compiled on the professor's dashboard.

Identifai.Me

Identifai.Me is a program that analyzes a user’s latest photo uploads on Facebook to generate insight into the user’s recent behaviour, habits, or trends. Using both Facebook’s Development API and Clarifai’s API (where we got the inspiration for our name), Identifai.Me makes it easy for users to seamlessely derive insighful social data at the click of one button. The simple one-step authentication method, coupled with the streamlined user interface promotes widespread use and adoption.
